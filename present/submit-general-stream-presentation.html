Submit a general stream presentation

Calls for presentations and workshops are now closed! Thanks for your submissions.

 

Conference organisers would like to invite people from across the Oceania region to submit an abstract so that your presentation can be considered for inclusion in the program.

Abstracts should be no more than 500 words. Submissions will be open until 31 August 4 September and the community will be given the opportunity to vote for presentations in a blind process. We anticipate informing presenters of their acceptance or otherwise by late September. For more detail, see the Call for Presentations and Workshops page.

Thank you for putting in the time to submit a presentation, and for helping us ensure the conference will be a success!

You can manage your submissions up until the closing date of 31 August 4 September from your account page and clicking on the 'My Submissions' tab.

 

Submissions are now closed.
